Privacy

When you use the contact form on this site, your name, email address, and message are sent to me so I can reply. I keep what you send only as long as I need to answer you and follow up on anything that comes out of that conversation. I don’t use your details for anything else, I don’t share them, and I don’t put you on any list.

The site uses Cloudflare Turnstile on the contact form to keep spam and abuse out. Turnstile works without cookies and without tracking you across other sites. Cloudflare’s own privacy notice covers how that works in more detail.

There are no advertising or tracking cookies on this site. If Cloudflare Web Analytics is enabled, it collects aggregate visit counts without cookies and without any personal data.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. If you want to know what I hold about you, or ask me to delete it, email me and I’ll sort it the same week.
