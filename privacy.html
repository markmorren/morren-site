Privacy.

There are no advertising or tracking cookies on this site. No log-ins. No analytics that follow you across the internet.

When you email hello@morren.uk, I keep what you send only as long as I need to reply and follow up. I don’t share it, and I don’t put you on any list.

The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. If Cloudflare Web Analytics is enabled at the hosting level, it collects aggregate visit counts — no cookies, no personal data.

If you’d like to know what I hold about you, or ask me to delete it, email me and I’ll sort it the same week.
