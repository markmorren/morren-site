About

I work in education in the UK and I build software in my evenings and weekends — mostly to solve problems I’ve run into during the day job. Everything here started with a real conversation: a teacher asking for something, a colleague needing a tool that didn’t exist, or me quietly losing petrol receipts down the side of my car seat.

I care about software that works on a school’s actual network, on the actual device a teacher has, in the actual five minutes they have between classes. If something I’ve built could help you, please get in touch.
